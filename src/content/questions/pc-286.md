---
id: 286
title: May someone be punished if what they speak is injurious to another?
slug: pc-286
categories:
  - rights
authorId: jdhall
---

Yes, if it can be proven in court that what has been alleged is false and resulting injury is demonstrable, this is called libel. Personal denigration of the character of another if proven true is not libelous and therefor not illegal.
