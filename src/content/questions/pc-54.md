---
id: 54
title: May a person be chosen who has just come into the United States, and who is a subject of some other country (that means, who is bound to obey the laws of some other country)?
slug: pc-54
categories:
  - legislature
authorId: jdhall
---

No. Any person, to be chosen a Member of our House of Representatives, must either have been born in the United States, or must have been a naturalized citizen for seven years before he is eligible.
