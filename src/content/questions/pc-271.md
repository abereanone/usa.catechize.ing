id: 271
title: Is there any recourse for the State, if the Courts are misinterpreting the Constitution or if laws are being enforced (or not enforced) despotically or prejudicially?
slug: pc-271
categories:
  - judiciary
authorId: jdhall
---

Yes, the Doctrine of the Lesser Magistrate demands that all elected offices - even smaller State or local magistrates - must defend the Constitution of the United States. Within their means and ability, they are able to resist tyranny by keeping their own oath to the Constitution.
