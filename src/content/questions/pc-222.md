title: Suppose some very important matter should arise while Congress is adjourned, can the President call them together?
slug: pc-222
categories:
  - executive
authorId: jdhall
---

Yes. He can call either both houses, or only one. If any law is to be made, both houses are required. if only a treaty or an appointment is to be made, the Senate only need be assembled.
