---
id: 119
title: Ought the people complain of having to pay taxes?
slug: pc-119
categories:
  - powers
authorId: jdhall
---

People ought to complain when taxes are unjust. However, if the benefits of taxation are distributed through the function of government to all classes of people equally, and all people are equal beneficiaries of government services to them (and they include only the services required of the government by the Constitution), then they should not complain about taxation.
