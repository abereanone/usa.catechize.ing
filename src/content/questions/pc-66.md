---
id: 66
title: Are all the Senators chosen at the same time, as are members of the House of Representatives?
slug: pc-66
categories:
  - legislature
authorId: jdhall
---

No. Only one third are chosen at a time in two year intervals, so that every two years one third of the number of the Senators see the expiration of their terms; but the same persons may again be chosen if the people wish to reelect them.
