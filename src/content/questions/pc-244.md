---
id: 244
title: What other cases are tried in the Courts?
slug: pc-244
categories:
  - judiciary
authorId: jdhall
---

All disputes in which the United States is a party; all disputes between one State and another State; all in which one of the States files suit against any person that is the Citizen of another State; all in which one of the States files suit against any person that is the Citizen of another of the States; all in which a Citizen of one State files suit against a Citizen of another State; all in which Citizens of one and the same State lay claim to land under grants of a different State; all in which one of the States files suit against a Citizen of a foreign country; and all in which Citizens of the United States, and Citizens of any other country file suit against each other. But not where Citizens of one State files suit against another State; or where Citizens or subjects of a foreign State file suit against one of the States of the Union.
