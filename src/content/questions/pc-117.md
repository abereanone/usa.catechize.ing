---
id: 117
title: Is there any other way that a tax may be unjust, aside from partiality?
slug: pc-117
categories:
  - powers
authorId: jdhall
---

Any wealth that is confiscated to redistribute to others is inherently unjust, and is theft. Likewise, any confiscation of wealth in order to finance governmental powers not granted them by the Constitution is inherently unjust.
