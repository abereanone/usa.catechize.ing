title: When the members of the House of Representatives meet to make the laws, are they all equal or does anyone preside over them?
slug: pc-60
categories:
  - legislature
authorId: jdhall
---

They choose one of their own number, whose duty it is to preside over them while they are assembled to do business, and to see that they proceed in a regular and orderly manner in doing their public duty. He is called their Speaker.
