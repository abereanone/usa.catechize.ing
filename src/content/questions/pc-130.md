---
id: 130
title: Are private banks, like that of the Federal Reserve, to be allowed to set monetary policy or print currency?
slug: pc-130
categories:
  - powers
authorId: jdhall
---

No. Only Congress may set monetary policy and print currency according to the Constitution. To allow this privilege to other bodies, is a dereliction of their duty and places the liberty of the citizens in the hands of private interest rather than in the hands of their duly elected representatives.
