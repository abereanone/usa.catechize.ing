title: You have said that no laws can be made for the United States but by Congress; may Congress make any laws they please?
slug: pc-100
categories:
  - powers
authorId: jdhall
---

No. Their power is limited by the Constitution. They have no power except that specifically granted them by the Constitution, which was designed to limit their power. Whatever power is not given to the federal government in the Constitution is reserved for the states and the people.
