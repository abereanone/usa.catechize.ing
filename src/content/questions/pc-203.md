title: What is the Presidential oath of office?
slug: pc-203
categories:
  - executive
authorId: jdhall
---

It is these words, "I do solemnly swear, that I will faithfully execute the office of President of the United States and will, to the best of my ability, preserve, protect, and defend the Constitution of the United States." By tradition, Presidents have often included at the end, "So help me God."
