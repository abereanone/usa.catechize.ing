---
id: 70
title: Does the Senate choose a Speaker, as the House of Representatives does?
slug: pc-70
categories:
  - legislature
authorId: jdhall
---

No. The person who is chosen by the people to be Vice President of the United States is made by the Constitution, President of the Senate; his duties are like those of the Speaker of the House of Representatives, except that he is not obliged to keep order in debate.
