title: Has he any great overriding duty?
slug: pc-226
categories:
  - executive
authorId: jdhall
---

Yes, he has one great, general, and constant duty in which all this power is put at his command, it is to take care that the laws shall be faithfully executed and enforced; that is, that whatever Congress orders shall be done, and that whoever disobeys their laws shall be punished.
